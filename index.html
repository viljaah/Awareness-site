The true cost of fast fashion

Fast fashion refers to the quick production of cheap clothing that mimics the latest trends. It's known for using low-cost materials and mass production. The goal is to meet the high demand for fashionable items at affordable prices.

While offering affordable clothing, it often involves production where workers are paid very low wages and work in poor conditions.
